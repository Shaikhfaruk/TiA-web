Choice and Competence in the Classroom: A Guide for Teachers

How can choice in the classroom impact student’s competence? Here’s what you need to know about the relationship between choice and student competence in the classroom. Identifying and discussing emotions can really help your kids gain some self-awareness into the way they are feeling and encourages healthy self-expression. Encourage them to think about why they are feeling that way – it will help them to realise the external factors that make them feel good or bad.
